ABSTRACT:
•The concept behind this project of “Metal Detector” is to ensure avoiding any illegal
or unauthorized entry of metallic objects, bombs, knives, guns within the luggage bags
of the person carrying them in public places like theatres, shopping malls, parks,
airports, hotels, railway stations etc.
•It is a low-cost metal detector using a transistor circuit.
•It is a in simple words A Colpitts oscillator working in the medium band frequency.
•This project gives an overview of metal detector working principle and applications.
